    Here is the current stat balance, from now on the balancing will use relative values.
    CON STR DEX INT WIS MR PR
    Mage 13.5 0.96 1.25 7.2 18.5 0.92 0.5
    Thief 15.2 1.15 2.15 4.95 14.5 0.5 0.75
    Warrior 21.0 1.95 1.25 3.5 12.0 0.5 1.25
    Modifiers and Effects, Reference sheet.
    • CON
      • Gives health per point.
      • Health regen increases.
      • [Heal]: Increased effect..
    • STR
      • [Warrior-Basic]: attack damage.
      • [Backstab]: Increased damage.
      • [Basic-Thief] Thief ranged damage.
      • [Magedagger]: Increased damage.
      • [Backstab]: Increased Damage.
      • [Telestrike]: Increased Duration.
      • [Jumpstrike]: Increased Damage.
      • [Passive: Mage]: Increases restored mana on-hit.
    • DEX
      • Increases movement speed.
      • Increases attack speed.
      • Adds physical resistance.
      • [Poison Arrow]: Increased Duration.
      • [Backstab]: Increased Damage.
    • INT
      • [Stun, Slow, Chaos]: Increased Damage.
      • [Chaos]: Increased Heal.
      • [Heal]: Increases Heal Slightly.
      • [Basic Mage]: Increases damage.
      • [Lifesteal] Increased heal, Increased damage.
      • [Hiddendagger]: Increased Damage.
      • [Stealth]: Increased Duration.
      • [Spellstrike]: Increased Damage.
      • [Mightystrikes]: Increased Damage.
      • [Shield]: Increased Hits.
    • WIS
      • Increased mana pool.
      • Increased mana regen.
      • Decreases cooldowns


    Additional class bonuses: - Warrior has increased haste duration. - Mage has decreased cooldowns and restores mana on-hit. - Thief has invisibility and backstab.
